About Me

With over 15 years of experience in jazz piano and vocals, I bring both technical expertise and creative passion to every lesson.

My journey in music began at the Berklee College of Music, where I received formal training in jazz voice and piano performance. After graduating with honors, I toured extensively with various jazz ensembles throughout Europe and North America, performing at renowned venues including the Blue Note in New York and Ronnie Scott's in London.

My teaching philosophy blends rigorous technical development with creative exploration. I believe that a strong foundation in breath support, vocal placement, and articulation is essential, but equally important is developing each student's unique artistic voice. Whether you're a beginner finding your sound or an experienced performer refining your craft, I create a supportive environment where you can take risks and grow.

Specializing in jazz vocals, I help students navigate the complexities of swing phrasing, improvisation, and the subtleties of jazz harmony. My approach integrates techniques from classical bel canto to contemporary vocal methods, ensuring versatility and vocal longevity.
